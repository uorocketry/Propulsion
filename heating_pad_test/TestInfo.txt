Test date: 03/07/2023

this test was performed to evaluate the effectivness of the oxidizer tank heater. The test was performed with a water filled tank with thermocouples placed at various points inside.
The data collected is used to predict how the heating pad will heat up nitrous for a coldflow/hotfire test in the winter.

Thermocouple placement:
top- through the top hole of tank
outer heating pad- placed on the outside of the heating pad
inside heating pad- placed on the inside of the tank, against the wall with the heating pad
bottom- placed inside the tank at the bottom most point

initial water temp = ~10C
heating pad specs:
2.16 kW
115V
heater was turned on and off every 1/5 second (0.2s)
